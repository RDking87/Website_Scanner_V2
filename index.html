Why your site isn't working

These are the most common reasons trade websites lose jobs every week.

No clear "call us" button
Visitors don't know where to click. If your phone number isn't obvious in 3 seconds, they're gone.
Doesn't work on mobile
Over 80% of people searching for trades are on their phone. A broken mobile site means lost jobs.
No reviews or trust signals
People hire who they trust. Without reviews or photos of your work, they choose someone else.
Loads too slowly
If your site takes more than 3 seconds to load, half your visitors leave before seeing anything.
Too much text, no direction
Walls of text confuse people. They should know what you do and how to call you in under 10 seconds.
Nobody knows your area
If people can't see where you work, they won't bother calling — they'll call someone who says it clearly.
